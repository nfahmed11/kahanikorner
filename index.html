Our Story

Kahani Korner was born at 3 a.m. — somewhere between rocking a baby and wondering why all Urdu bilingual books cost an arm and a leg and looked so boring. We wanted stories that sounded like home and looked like our kids. So we created cozy, activity-rich books that invite Urdu into everyday family life, one little kahani at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What age range are the resources for?

Our books are designed for children ages 0–8, The Kahani Times is for preteens and up, but language learners of all ages enjoy them! Everything is meant to grow as you go!

Do I need to know Urdu to read them?

Not at all! Every book includes English, Urdu script, and Roman Urdu (Urdu written in English letters) so anyone can read along, regardless of fluency. The QR codes included also have audio read alouds so you can listen or read along.

Where do you ship?

We currently ship worldwide! Free shipping is available for all orders within the US. International rates are calculated at checkout.

What comes in the subscription box?

Each monthly box is a surprise theme packed with a new bilingual akhbar (newspaper), stickers, activities and more to keep you engaged and learning Urdu.

Can I buy in bulk for my classroom?

Yes! We love supporting educators. Please contact us for special bulk pricing and lesson plan resources.
